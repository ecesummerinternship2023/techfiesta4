About us

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) has excelled since 1994, offering an accredited B.E. program and introducing the M.E. (Applied Electronics) program in 2003. With three gold medallists and over 80 rank holders, the department has consistently achieved academic excellence. Faculty members, with eleven holding Ph.D.s, contribute significantly to research with numerous publications. Currently, 10 staff members are pursuing doctoral programs.The department actively fosters research through National-level events, including four conferences, 19 symposiums, and 16 workshops, showcasing a dynamic commitment to advancing knowledge in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
